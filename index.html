Prevention Methods
Make sure not to use homemade fireworks.
Never allow young children to play with fireworks.
Make sure there is adult supervision.
Never place any part of your body directly over a firework, even when trying to light the fuse.
Never re-light or pick up fireworks that have not ignited fully.
Only light one firework at a time.
Never set fireworks off in metal or glass containers.
Keep a bucket of water or a garden hose near by and always drench the spent firework in water to prevent fires when throwing them out.
Brown paper packaged fireworks are made for professional displays and should not be bought by general public.
Only buy and use fireworks if it is legal in your area. The guildlines can change from city to city so check what the guidelines are for your area.
Make sure everyone is a safe distance from the display and it is always good to have earmuffs or ear plugs.
If any injuries do happen, call 911 immediately.
